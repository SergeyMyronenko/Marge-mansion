About Us

Merge Farm Life: Mansion Story is a captivating and relaxing merge puzzle game where players combine items to create new objects and expand their farm and mansion. Embark on a journey to restore an old, abandoned mansion to its former glory while cultivating a lush farm...The game features fun merging mechanics, beautiful visuals, and an immersive story. Perfect for casual gamers, it combines creativity and strategy with a satisfying progression system. Explore charming environments, solve puzzles, and create the ultimate farm and mansion experience!
