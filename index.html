FAQ

  • How do I start the game?

    Download the app, install it, and press 'Play' to begin merging and building your farm and mansion.

  • Is the game free?

    Yes, Merge Farm Life: Mansion Story is free to download and play, with optional in-app purchases.

  • Can I play without an internet connection?

    Yes, you can enjoy the game offline, making it perfect for playing anywhere, anytime.

  • What are the boosters?

    Boosters help speed up your progress by instantly clearing items or unlocking new areas. You can earn or purchase boosters to make challenging puzzles easier.

  • How do I unlock new levels and areas?

    Complete puzzles, merge items, and expand your mansion to unlock new areas and levels with additional challenges.

  • Can I play with friends?

    Yes, you can share your progress on social media, compete with friends on leaderboards, and exchange tips on how to complete challenging puzzles.
